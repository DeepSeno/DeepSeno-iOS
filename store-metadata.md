### 3. Description

#### English (App Store)

DeepSeno is the mobile companion for the DeepSeno desktop app — a voice-powered second brain that captures, transcribes, and organizes everything you say. Record audio, snap photos, shoot videos, or jot down text memos on your phone, then sync it all to your desktop for AI-powered processing. No cloud required — everything stays on your local network.

**CAPTURE ANYTHING, ANYWHERE**
Your phone is always with you. DeepSeno turns it into a powerful capture device:
- Record voice memos with one tap
- Take photos or batch-capture multiple images
- Shoot video clips (up to 3 minutes)
- Write quick text memos
- Import files from your device (audio, video, PDFs, images, documents)

All captured content is automatically queued and synced to your desktop over your local Wi-Fi network.

**AI-POWERED ASSISTANT**
Chat with your AI assistant to query your entire knowledge base. Ask questions about your meetings, conversations, and notes — the AI retrieves relevant context from your recordings and provides accurate, sourced answers. Streaming responses mean you see answers as they are generated.

**DAILY & WEEKLY BRIEFINGS**
Stay on top of your day with AI-generated summaries:
- Daily summaries of your recordings and conversations
- Weekly reports for a broader view
- Extracted to-do items you can check off directly
- Key decisions, meetings, contacts, and action items pulled from your voice data

**BROWSE YOUR SOURCES**
Access your full recording library from your phone:
- Filter by type: voice, video, document, image, or text
- Full-text search across all transcripts
- View detailed transcripts with speaker identification and timestamps
- See meeting notes, key topics, and extracted information
- Browse image galleries for photo-based recordings

**EFFORTLESS PAIRING**
Connect to your desktop in seconds:
- Scan a QR code displayed on your desktop app
- Or use automatic device discovery (Bonjour/mDNS) on your local network
- Manual connection option with host, port, and token
- Persistent connection with automatic reconnect

**PRIVACY FIRST**
- All AI processing happens locally on your desktop — nothing leaves your network
- No cloud accounts, no subscriptions, no data collection
- Communication between phone and desktop uses your local Wi-Fi only
- Your voice data, transcripts, and personal information stay entirely under your control

**REAL-TIME SYNC**
- WebSocket connection for instant updates
- See new recordings appear as they are processed on your desktop
- Pipeline progress tracking shows processing status in real-time
- Upload queue with automatic retry and offline support

**BILINGUAL INTERFACE**
Full English and Chinese (simplified) language support throughout the app.

DeepSeno requires the DeepSeno desktop app (macOS or Windows) running on the same local network. The desktop app handles all AI processing including speech-to-text transcription, speaker identification, text optimization, information extraction, and RAG-based conversational queries.
