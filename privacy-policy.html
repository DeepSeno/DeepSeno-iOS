DeepSeno Privacy Policy

思维匣子隐私政策

Effective Date: 2026-03-16

DeepSeno ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile companion application for the DeepSeno desktop app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the DeepSeno mobile app handles your data.

In short: DeepSeno does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All data stays on your devices and your local network. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no cloud services.

1. Data Collection

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, or other personal data

Data Created Locally

The app creates the following data, which is stored only on your device:

  • Audio recordings — Voice memos you choose to record
  • Photos and videos — Media you choose to capture or import
  • Text memos — Notes you write within the app
  • Connection settings — Your desktop app's network address and authentication token, stored in local device storage (UserDefaults / SharedPreferences)
  • Upload queue — A local database of pending uploads, stored on-device using SwiftData (iOS) or Room (Android)
  • Cached data — Recordings and briefings cached locally for offline access

2. Data Transmission

DeepSeno transmits data only between your phone and your desktop computer over your local Wi-Fi network. Specifically:

Data Type Destination Protocol
Audio, photos, videos, text memos Your desktop (local network only) HTTP (local Wi-Fi)
AI chat questions Your desktop (local network only) HTTP + SSE (local Wi-Fi)
Real-time sync events Your desktop (local network only) WebSocket (local Wi-Fi)
Device discovery Local network broadcast Bonjour/mDNS/NSD

No data is ever sent to our servers, third-party services, cloud storage, or any destination outside your local network. The app does not connect to the internet.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions, each used solely for the stated purpose:

Permission Purpose
Microphone Recording voice memos that you initiate
Camera Capturing photos and videos that you initiate
Local Network Communicating with the DeepSeno desktop app on your Wi-Fi network
Photo Library (iOS) / Storage (Android) Importing existing photos and videos you select

All permissions are optional. The app will function with reduced capabilities if you decline any permission. You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

DeepSeno does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are:

  • No analytics services (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage providers

5. AI Processing

All AI and machine learning processing (speech-to-text transcription, speaker identification, text optimization, information extraction, and conversational queries) is performed entirely by the DeepSeno desktop application running on your local computer. The mobile app itself performs no AI processing. Your voice data and transcripts are never sent to any external AI service.

6. Data Security

  • Communication between the mobile app and desktop app is authenticated using a token-based system
  • Connection credentials are stored securely in local device storage
  • Data transfer occurs exclusively over your local network
  • The app does not store sensitive data in logs or temporary files beyond what is needed for the upload queue

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Captured media files are temporary and are deleted from the phone after successful upload to your desktop
  • Connection settings persist until you manually disconnect or uninstall the app
  • Cached data (recordings list, briefings) is stored locally and can be cleared by disconnecting or uninstalling
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

8. Children's Privacy

DeepSeno is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, this is not applicable in practice.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@deepseno.com
  • Website: https://deepseno.com

AI 对话问题 您的桌面电脑（仅限本地网络） HTTP + SSE（本地 Wi-Fi）
实时同步事件 您的桌面电脑（仅限本地网络） WebSocket（本地 Wi-Fi）
设备发现 本地网络广播 Bonjour/mDNS/NSD
